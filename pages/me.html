Keenan Elijah Dailey ( /KEY-nan/ ; born July 7, 1995) is an American Graphic Designer, Filmmaker, Illustrator, Producer, Singer-Songwriter and Actor
studying Graphic Design at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.Dailey is both an artist and researcher of aesthetic. Through his artistic exploration Dailey seeks
to uncover and unearth the hidden potential within humanistic empathy by way of the creation of sensorial experiences of any and all medium. Dailey has not shied away from
any means within the digital space in terms of getting his point across weather it be through Graphic Design, Film Production, Music Production, Videogame Development
, Digital Interactivity and so on and so forth.
